Depop Integration Coming in May

Aditya Payanadan | Integration Specialist
April 10, 2025

We're thrilled to announce that Depop, one of the fastest-growing fashion marketplaces in the world, will soon be integrated into the Acumen platform! Scheduled for release in May 2025, this integration will allow our users to automatically create and manage Depop listings directly from their Acumen dashboard.

Why Depop?

Depop has rapidly become one of the most influential marketplaces for fashion, vintage, and unique items, with over 30 million registered users worldwide. The platform is particularly popular among Gen Z shoppers and fashion enthusiasts looking for one-of-a-kind pieces and sustainable fashion options.

Many of our users have been requesting Depop integration, especially those who sell vintage clothing, designer items, and handcrafted fashion pieces. By bringing Depop into our ecosystem of supported marketplaces, we're expanding your reach to millions of fashion-forward buyers.

"Depop has been on our integration roadmap for some time, and we're excited to finally bring this highly-requested marketplace to our platform. This integration represents our commitment to supporting sellers across diverse and specialised marketplaces."

— Aditya Payanadan, Integration Specialist at Acumen

Key Features of the Depop Integration

Our Depop integration will include a comprehensive set of features designed to streamline your selling experience:

  • One-Click Listing Creation: Generate Depop-optimised listings with the same AI-powered tools you use for other marketplaces
  • Specialised Fashion Tags: Our AI will automatically suggest relevant style tags and fashion categories popular on Depop
  • Cross-Platform Inventory Management: Sync inventory levels across all your marketplaces, including Depop
  • Sales Analytics: Track performance metrics for your Depop listings alongside your other marketplace data
  • Bulk Operations: Upload and manage multiple Depop listings simultaneously
  • Automatic Price Recommendations: Get AI-powered pricing suggestions based on similar items selling on Depop

Development Progress

Our development team has been working closely with Depop's API team to ensure a seamless integration. We've already completed the core functionality for listing creation and management, and we're now finalising the specialized features that make Depop unique, such as style tags, fashion categories, and the platform's specific image requirements.

We're also fine-tuning our AI description generator to better align with Depop's style and audience expectations. The fashion-forward nature of Depop requires more attention to trends and style terminology, which our AI is being specially trained to understand.

Timeline and Availability

Here's our current timeline for the Depop integration:

  • April 10-20: Final API integration and testing
  • April 21-30: Beta testing with select users
  • May 1-10: Refinements based on beta feedback
  • May 15: Full release to all Acumen users

The Depop integration will be available to all users on Plus, Pro, and Premium plans at no additional cost. Users on our Free plan will have limited access with the ability to post up to 3 Depop listings per month.

Optimising Your Depop Strategy

Depop differs from other marketplaces in several important ways, and we're committed to helping our users succeed on this unique platform. To support your Depop selling journey, we'll be releasing a series of resources along with the integration:

  • A comprehensive guide to Depop selling best practices
  • Webinars focused on fashion photography and styling for Depop
  • Templates optimised for different Depop categories
  • Case studies of successful Depop sellers

What This Means for Your Business

For sellers of fashion, vintage items, accessories, and unique products, the addition of Depop to our supported marketplaces opens up significant opportunities to expand your customer base. Depop's highly engaged community of style-conscious buyers represents a valuable audience segment that complements other marketplaces like eBay and Facebook Marketplace.

With this integration, you'll be able to:

  • Reach a younger, fashion-forward demographic
  • Sell items that perform particularly well on Depop (vintage, unique, trendy pieces)
  • Save time by managing all your marketplace listings from one dashboard
  • Apply Acumen's AI-powered listing optimisation to your Depop presence

Looking Ahead

The Depop integration is just one part of our ongoing commitment to expanding Acumen's marketplace support. We're continuously evaluating new platforms and enhancing our existing integrations to ensure you have the most comprehensive multi-channel selling solution available.

We're excited to see how our users leverage the Depop integration to grow their businesses and reach new customers. As always, we'll be closely monitoring your feedback after launch to identify opportunities for further enhancements and optimisations.
