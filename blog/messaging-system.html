New Messaging System Coming Soon

Giordan Masen | Lead Developer
April 17, 2025

We're excited to announce that our team is developing a comprehensive messaging system that will allow direct communication between sellers across all integrated marketplaces. This highly requested feature is currently in active development and set to launch in late May 2025.

Unified Communication Hub

One of the biggest challenges sellers face today is managing conversations across multiple platforms. Our new messaging system aims to solve this problem by creating a central hub where you can interact with all your customer messages from different marketplaces in one place.

No more switching between apps or websites to respond to potential buyers. Whether a message comes from eBay, Facebook Marketplace, or any of our other integrated platforms, you'll be able to view and respond to it directly from your Acumen dashboard.

Key Features

Our messaging system will include several powerful features designed to streamline your communication workflow:

  • Unified Inbox: All messages from all platforms in one centralied location
  • Auto-Categorisation: Messages will be automatically sorted by platform, item, and conversation status
  • Template Responses: Create and save template messages for common questions
  • AI-Assisted Replies: Get suggestions for professional responses based on customer inquiries
  • Conversation History: Complete message history for each buyer, organied by listing
  • Mobile Notifications: Receive alerts for new messages on your mobile device

Current Development Status

Our development team has completed the core messaging infrastructure and is currently focused on integrating with each individual marketplace API. We've successfully connected with eBay's messaging system and are now working on Facebook Marketplace integration.

The user interface design is complete, and we're conducting initial usability testing with a small group of beta users. Early feedback has been overwhelmingly positive, with testers particularly appreciating the streamlined workflow and time savings.

"The messaging system has already saved me hours of work. Being able to respond to all customer inquiries from one dashboard is a game-changer for my business."

— Beta Tester, Professional Seller

Implementation Timeline

Here's our current development schedule:

  • April 17-30: Complete Facebook Marketplace integration
  • May 1-14: Integrate remaining marketplaces and finalise notification system
  • May 15-25: Extended beta testing and bug fixes
  • May 26: Full release to all Acumen users

Accessibility Across All Plans

The basic version of our messaging system will be available to all Acumen users, including those on the free tier. Premium features like AI-assisted replies and advanced templates will be exclusive to our paid subscription plans.

Looking Forward

This messaging system represents a significant step in our mission to create the ultimate all-in-one platform for online sellers. By centralising communications alongside our existing tools for listings creation and management, we're making it easier than ever to run a successful e-commerce business.

We're committed to continuous improvement, and we'll be closely monitoring user feedback after launch to identify areas for enhancement. Future updates may include bulk messaging capabilities, automated follow-ups, and additional AI tools to help you communicate more effectively with customers.

Stay tuned for more updates as we get closer to the official launch. We can't wait for you to experience how this new system will transform your selling workflow.
