Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the AI in describing my products?

Our AI has been trained on millions of product listings and can identify thousands of items with 95%+ accuracy. For best results, we recommend uploading multiple clear photos from different angles.

Which marketplaces does Acumen support?

Acumen currently supports eBay, Facebook Marketplace, and Grailed with more platforms being added regularly. Our roadmap includes Etsy, Poshmark, and Mercari in the next quarter.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can change your subscription plan at any time. Changes will be reflected at the start of your next billing cycle.
